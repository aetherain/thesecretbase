Helios Rising Heroes - Red South Street Star Cafe Short Skit Translation

Jay Kidman and Gray Reverse from East Sector

Gray : …

Jay : Oi, aren’t you Gray?

Gray : ! Jay-san?

Jay : Unexpected, right? To meet at this kind of place on our day off.

Gray : I was surprised…what are you doing here?

Jay: Aah, I’m buying donuts, I buy them sometimes because I like the donuts here.

Gray : So it’s like that…

Jay: With that said, why are you…Ah no, wait a sec. Let me try to guess.

Jay: You like cupcakes, right? You came to buy the newly released flavour…how was it?

Gray: That, that’s right…! Jay-san, to think that you remember my favourite things…!

Jay: Hahaha, of course. Lately if I see any cupcakes, your face comes to mind.

Jay: Do you always come here since before?

Gray: Yes. The cupcakes here, although the inside is fluffy, there are crunchy parts as well; the best part is that the cupcake texture is different from other stores’.

Gray: The cream on top tastes perfect, and because the sweetness is not overwhelming, I never get tired eating them…

Gray: Ah…excuse me. I’m getting excited by myself.

Jay: No, I understand your feelings. Well, why don’t I buy some cupcake to take home as well today.

Gray: Whoa…then, I’ll take away some donuts as well!
